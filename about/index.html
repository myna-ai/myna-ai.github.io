So, how does it work?
Most language learning apps today rely on popular language models like ChatGPT and Google Gemini. However, they process your data through their servers, acting as intermediaries, which is why they typically charge a monthly subscription fee. Myna, on the other hand, processes data directly on your phone and connects straight to OpenAI or GeminiAI. This eliminates the need to pay for middlemen. The only catch is that you need to enter your own API keys from OpenAI or GeminiAI.
It's cheap, but how cheap?
We have been rigorously testing the app using our API keys for the past few months. Multiple team members have been sharing the same key, and our total expenditure so far? Just $0.16! This amount may vary for different users, but it is unlikely to be significant. With a one-time payment of $10 to unlock in-app purchases (no monthly subscription) and a few cents each month for ChatGPT or GeminiAI API usage, you can learn a new language for less than $15.
