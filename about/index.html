Why did we create Myna?
There are so many language learning apps out there, do we really need another one? We didn't set out just to compete with the already great options available. However, we found that many existing apps are quite pricey or filled with annoying advertisements. Is there a way to learn languages affordably while still focusing on the most important aspectâ€”learning by communicating? That's how we began brainstorming for Myna.
So how does it work?
Most language learning apps today rely on popular language models like ChatGPT and Google Gemini. However, they process your data through their servers, acting as intermediaries, which is why they typically charge a monthly subscription fee. Myna, on the other hand, processes data directly on your phone and connects straight to OpenAI or GeminiAI. This eliminates the need to pay for middlemen. The only catch is that you need to enter API keys from OpenAI or GeminiAI.
Myna is built to emulate real-life interactions
Myna offers tons of scenarios designed to assist with daily life, from ordering food to navigating public transportation. These practical interactions help you develop language skills applicable to real-world activities, such as small talk, asking for directions, or handling business meetings. By engaging with these interactive scenarios, you enhance your vocabulary, grammar, cultural understanding, and situational awareness, becoming a more confident and effective communicator.
